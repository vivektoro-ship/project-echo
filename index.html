FAQ

Who can report an issue?

Anyone doing the visit can raise a report. A manager can report on anyone's visit too — no restriction.

Who can nudge or respond to an issue?

The person doing the visit, plus their manager.

If I go back mid-report, does it save?

Yes — it's kept as a draft. Edit and submit it anytime.

What do I have to fill in?

For agents, just a description — and even that's auto-filled, e.g. Issue in <page> · <module> · latest API.
